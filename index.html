Summary

Kavita, meaning Stories, is a full fledged, fast manga server. Built from the ground up to serve as a self-hosted, open-source manga server. Built with Role-based Security, User Authentication & Preferences to create a solution that feels right.

Features

Browse your Manga

See all your manga, properly named in one place

Search

Get to your manga as fast as you can type

Metadata

Initial support for metadata with more detailed information coming soon

Ratings and Reviews

Built-in ratings and reviews per Manga

Mixed Volume and Chapters

Chapters and volumes integrated next to each other

User & Library Management from anywhere

User-based roles, authentication and library management right from the app

And more!

  • Zip, CBZ Support (RAR, CBR coming)
  • Multi-zip mapping to volumes, seemless to end user
  • Grouping of volumes/chapters by a parsed Series name, not folders
  • Cross Platform with no dependencies - Everything in the box
  • Responsive and Fast!
  • Full text search to quickly find your manga
  • Break up your Manga collection into Libraries each with user access rules
  • Create users and share your collection with your friends
  • Rate and review your Manga right in app
